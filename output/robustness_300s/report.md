# Robustness: 300s CCZ Candidate

Pre-declared checks: day-shifted cross-feature placebo; DM/MCS with p-values corrected for the 672-cell search; horizon extension {40,60,90,120}; lookback alternatives ccz_short, ccz_dense, ccz_long.

## Interpretation

The robustness battery weakens the positive claim. The 300s/CCZ family still
looks like a real candidate because the unshifted cross OFI usually beats the
day-shifted placebo in raw R2 and PnL, and because 21 / 28 cells beat own OFI
after Bonferroni correction over the 672-cell discovery grid. But the decisive
controls do not pass: 0 / 28 cells beat cross-return history after the same
correction, and 0 / 28 cells beat the shifted-placebo cross OFI after the same
correction. The extended-horizon test also fails the R2 controls.

Conclusion: keep the 300s/CCZ result as a pre-registered holdout candidate, not
as a proven generalisation.
